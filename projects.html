Forecasting US unemployment rate on Python

This project is a replication of a well-known paper on national unemployment rate forecast. I have decided to replicate this paper to build similar type of model for Idaho economy. The original model was constructed on STATA statistical software. In this project, I will go through the theory and construct the model on Python.

Creating SQL database from scratch: Tax income database

In this project, I cleaned, manipulated, and migrated 20 years of Income Tax datasets for tax filers into a relational database system on PostgreSQL. This project illustrates my thought process on building database from scratch on a simple example. The project involves lots of data cleaning from raw Excel spreadsheets on Python, ERD designing process, and PostgreSQL codes for data importing.

Sales and online sales tax forecasing with ARIMA and regression model

This projects deals with prediction difficulties of short series (i.e. online sales tax) through its theoretical correlation with another series with long history (i.e. sales tax). I built SARIMA model on highly seasonal sales tax data and built simple regression model to forecast online sales tax.
